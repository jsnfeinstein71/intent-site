Why Intent exists

Created from a real need, not a feature checklist.

I created Intent because I wanted a faster way to capture thoughts. Not folders. Not confirmation boxes. Not bloated workflows. Just tap, speak, and move on.

Intent is built around speed, clarity, and forgiveness. It helps you get something out of your head and into motion before the moment disappears.

It also matters beyond capture. Intent is the front door to a broader direction: smarter routing, deeper context, and a future connection to Persona Lite.

Core ideas

Simple on purpose.

Capture fast

Voice or text input with minimal friction, so a thought becomes an action before it disappears.

Route later

Stage items, share them, send them, or move them into the workflow that makes sense.

Built with forgiveness

Undo, simple gestures, and fast handling so speed does not punish mistakes.

Local-first practicality

Intent starts from a grounded idea: an action inbox should feel fast, private, and dependable.
