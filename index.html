What Is Intent?

Intent gives you a clean place to catch something now and handle it later. No cluttered workflow. No unnecessary steps. Just quick capture and practical control.

Use it for thoughts, errands, reminders, follow-ups, quick notes, or anything else that needs to get out of your head before it disappears.

Built for actual life

Intent is designed for speed, forgiveness, and everyday usefulness — not for bloated process or feature overload.

Core Features

Intent is deliberately focused. Everything about it is built around making capture feel quick, natural, and dependable.

How It Works

Intent keeps the process simple so the app does not get in your way.

1. Capture it

The moment something matters, put it into Intent by voice or text.

2. Keep moving

Do not stop to organize your whole life in the moment. Just save it and move on.

3. Review later

Come back when you are ready and decide what should happen next.

4. Route it

Delete it, stage it in notifications, share it, or send it into your next workflow.

Why I Created Intent

I wanted a faster way to capture thoughts without fighting through clutter, confirmation boxes, and overbuilt workflow.

I wanted something immediate: tap, speak, save, move on.

Intent comes from that need. It is built to feel practical, fast, and forgiving enough to use every day.
