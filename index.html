Built to last as its own product

What Is Intent?

Intent is a fast local inbox for actions. It gives you a clean place to catch something now and decide what to do with it later.

That might be a thought, a reminder, a to-do, a follow-up, or a command. The point is not complexity. The point is speed, clarity, and momentum.

Designed for actual use

Intent is not trying to be everything. It is designed to do one important job extremely well: help you capture quickly and keep moving.

Why Intent Exists

I created Intent because I wanted a faster way to capture thoughts without dealing with clutter, confirmation boxes, folder structures, or account friction.

I wanted something immediate: tap, speak, save, move on.

Intent is built around that principle. It is meant to feel practical, forgiving, and fast enough to stay out of your way.

Core Product Qualities

Intent is deliberately focused. It is built to be useful now, not overloaded with unnecessary process.

Current direction

Fast capture

Capture by voice or text with minimal friction so an idea becomes an action before it disappears.

Route later

Capture first. Decide what to do with it afterward through sharing, staging, notification workflows, or future routing paths.

Built-in forgiveness

Undo and simple handling patterns help speed feel safe rather than punishing.

Local-first feel

Intent starts from a grounded philosophy: an action inbox should feel close, dependable, and ready when you need it.

Real-life utility

The app is designed for reminders, errands, follow-ups, quick notes, operational tasks, and day-to-day action capture.

Strong on its own

Intent is meant to stand as a lasting product in its own right, not a temporary step toward something else.

Where Intent Fits

Intent lives within a broader ecosystem, but it is important to understand it on its own terms first: Intent is a complete product for fast local action capture.

Beyond that, it also connects naturally to a larger family of systems around personal intelligence and governed AI.

Intent

The action surface: fast, practical, local-first, and durable as a standalone product.

Persona Lite

A future consumer-facing form of Persona designed for personal use, built to be powerful rather than watered down.

StarBridge Persona

The governed runtime-based Persona system intended for critical, sensitive, and auditable environments.

How they relate

Intent can remain what it is indefinitely, while also serving as a natural gateway into the broader Persona direction.

One ecosystem, distinct roles

Intent is not a placeholder. Persona Lite is not a replacement for Intent. StarBridge Persona is not the same product for a different audience. Each has its own role.

Intent and Persona Lite

Intent serves as a natural gateway to Persona Lite, but it does not exist just to lead somewhere else.

Persona Lite is the consumer-facing form of Persona: personal, powerful, and designed for everyday use. It is not simply a weaker version of Persona, and it is not a replacement for StarBridge Persona.

StarBridge Persona remains the governed runtime-based system for environments that require trust, control, and auditability.

Intent

The enduring capture surface. Fast, focused, and useful whether or not a user ever moves beyond it.

Persona Lite

The personal intelligence layer that can grow around capture and extend it with context, memory, and assistance.

Consumer-facing power

“Lite” refers to deployment context, not weakness. The goal is a personal form of Persona, not a stripped-down toy.

Governed Persona

StarBridge Persona remains the path for sensitive systems that require managed trust, runtime control, and auditing.

Intent can live forever

Persona Lite may expand the ecosystem, but Intent keeps its own lasting value as a clean action-capture product.
